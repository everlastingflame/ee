[[Discrete Distributions]]

Used to describe the probability that $X$ events occur on a time interval $t$ where an event with rate $\lambda$ occurs at any time unit. 
$$
\begin{align}
&\text{Poisson}(\lambda t) \\
&f(x) = \frac{1}{x!}(\lambda t)^xe^{-\lambda t}, \text{for} \, x=0,1,\dots \\
&m(s) = e^{\lambda t(\exp (s)-1)}
\end{align}
$$
